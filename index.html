About Me

When I used Google drive for the first time, I wanted to explore the bounds of that app; the controls, the settings, and the features it came with. This quickly made me realize how much I knew about the app in such a little time that I was teaching my peers about changing the color of their drive folders. My curiosity for technology has grown immensely since I came to Canada in grade 7.

I enjoy exploring new technologies that I come across to familiarize myself, and gain knowledge about its unique features.

My name is Riddhima. I am a Computer Science student at Toronto Metropolitan University; currently in third year. I have a passion for Full Stack Web Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science in fields of health care, robotics and business.
